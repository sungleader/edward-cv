About Edward SUNG

Edward Sung is an interdisciplinary researcher in Sydney, Australia, specializing in machine/deep learning and econometrics. He aims to solve various energy issues through modelling. Additionally, he is pursuing a PhD at the University of Technology Sydney (UTS) as a recipient of the prestigious UTS Research Excellence Scholarship.

With over a decade of experience in corporate finance and strategic planning, Edward has worked with leading global energy and financial companies. As an entrepreneur, he manages an investment scheme/trust, utilising his expertise to uncover opportunities in the real estate market. He holds a top-tier education from the U.S., including Ivy League credentials, and is fluent in both English and Korean.

His research areas encompass not only energy but also a wide range of fields, including finance and environment. His expertise includes energy and financial modelling, corporate strategic planning, and in-depth financial and operational analysis. These capabilities have established him as a versatile and insightful professional in both academic research and corporate practice.
